The Venue

Redbridge Town Hall - Lambourne Room

How to get there

The venue is situated next to the Ilford shopping centre (The Exchange)

The venue has multiple halls so please look for Lambourne room which is located on lower floor.

Accessibility

Please drop any disabled, elder and prams to the venue before looking for parking
Lifts are located near the enterance



Parking

There is no free parking available at the venue but there plenty of car parks nearby
